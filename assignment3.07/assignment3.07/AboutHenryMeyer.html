About Me

My name is Henry Meyer, I use any pronouns, and I am the CEO of PT@OT

PT@OT (Public Transport at Oakland Tech), founded in 2023 by me, is a non-profit orginization to help increase the usage of public transportation specifically at Oakland Tech, to reward students who do. While it should not be on the individual to reduce the countries carbon footprint, we can still do everything we can do help out, and reducing usage of cars can help that, and hopefully promote legislature that improves public transit in our country. I've been interested in this topic for around a year now, after seeing other people talk about this issue and potential solutions.

I am 17 years old, and I am a senior at Oakland Technical High School. I've been a varsity rower for 3 years where I've earned a 2nd place Scholastic National Medal in the Varsity Lightweight 2x event, I love cats and have 2 of my own, Franny and Whiskers who I've had for 7 years now, and I love listening to music, my favorite artists are Car Seat Headrest, Underscores, Jane Remover, and Six Impala.
